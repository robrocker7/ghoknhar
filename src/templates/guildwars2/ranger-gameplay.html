// Thoughts:

The Char Ranger was my first profession in Guild Wars 2 so I had the learning curve of figuring out the UI and the feel of the game. Even with these obstacles I feel that the Ranger played really well!

The combat felt really great and weighty, the short bow was my favoriate weapon for questing cause of the single target DPS. The long bow seems to be more for AoE damage.

I only played levels 1-8 but in that time the pets seems to die almost instantly and then would roam around useless for a bit during larger event battles. For 1 or 2 mobs the pet did great holding aggro while I put out the DPS, however the damage output of the pet was rather low.
